Status Labels

Label OK Label Warning Label Critical

Use span.label to create a label. UX Aspects offers a wide range of label & outline label colors. Set the color of the label using one of the following classes:

General Labels:

.label-primary or .label-accent or .label-alternate1 or .label-alternate2 or .label-alternate3 or .label-vibrant1 or .label-vibrant2 or .label-grey1 or .label-grey2 or .label-grey3 or .label-grey4 or .label-grey5

Outline Labels:

.label-outline-primary or .label-outline-accent or .label-outline-alternate1 or .label-outline-alternate2 or .label-outline-alternate3 or .label-outline-vibrant1 or .label-outline-vibrant2 or .label-outline-grey1 or .label-outline-grey2 or .label-outline-grey3 or .label-outline-grey4 or .label-outline-grey5

Status Labels:

.label-ok or .label-warning or .label-critical

If the colour of the label is being used to convey meaning, include an aria-label attribute to describe this information to users of assistive technologies.
